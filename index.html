What is SDG 9?

Sustainable Development Goal 9 focuses on building resilient infrastructure, promoting sustainable industrialization, and fostering innovation. This goal recognizes that reliable infrastructure and sustainable industries are the backbone of economic development and human prosperity.

SDG 9 is critical for achieving many other Sustainable Development Goals. It encompasses:

  • ✓ Developing reliable, sustainable, and resilient infrastructure
  • ✓ Promoting inclusive and sustainable industrialization
  • ✓ Fostering innovation and technological development
  • ✓ Increasing access to ICT and internet
  • ✓ Supporting domestic technology development

Key Targets

9.1

Reliable Infrastructure

Develop reliable, sustainable, and resilient infrastructure to support economic development and human well-being, with emphasis on affordable access.

9.2

Sustainable Industrialization

Promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization and foster innovation in all countries, particularly in developing nations.

9.3

Small Business Support

Increase access to ICT and provide internet access with a focus on supporting micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises and entrepreneurs.

9.4

Clean Industries

Upgrade infrastructure and retrofit industries to make them sustainable and resource-efficient, with increased adoption of clean technologies.

9.5

Innovation & Research

Enhance scientific research and industrial technological capabilities, particularly in developing countries and least developed countries.

9.c

ICT Access

Significantly increase access to ICT and provide free internet access in least developed countries and small island states.

Current Challenges

  • Financing Gap: Insufficient capital for infrastructure projects
  • Digital Divide: Unequal access to technology and internet
  • Technology Transfer: Limited sharing of advanced technologies
  • Sustainability: Need for environmentally friendly infrastructure
  • Capacity Building: Skills shortage in developing regions
